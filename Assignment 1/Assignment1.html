Why I Joined the AltSchoolAfrica Program

Ever since I was little, I've been fascinated by tech like I said before. Fast forward to my 300-level in university, I decided it was time to take this love for tech seriously. Armed with nothing but my laptop and YouTube tutorials, I dove headfirst into the world of tech and I've never looked back. I'd been seeing AltSchool Africa on twitter for a while, and it always seemed like this amazing place for tech. Then one day, I saw a scholarship opportunity shared by Ruby Lauren, and it felt like the universe was giving me a nudge. I decided to go for it, and after applying and writing the entrance assessment, I did a deep dive into what AltSchool was all about. When I saw that I got a half-scholarship, I was so excited, It felt like destiny, and my journey with AltSchool has been nothing short of amazing so far. The mentorship, the learning, the community it's all been perfect. I can't wait to crush my goals, build awesome projects, and make my mark in tech. AltSchool isn't just a school for me; it's the place where my dreams are turning into reality. I joined the AltSchoolAfrica program to gain a solid foundation in frontend engineering. AltSchool offers a unique, practical, and mentorship driven approach to learning. I wanted to learn in a structured environment where I could gain hands-on experience, meet like-minded peers, and be equipped with the skills required to tackle real world problems.
